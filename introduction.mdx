This documentation covers the open-source projects and developer tools published by Jacob P. Evans. ## What's covered

The documentation is organized into three main areas: getting started guides that walk you through initial setup, practical workflow guides for common tasks, and a reference section with answers to frequently asked questions. Each section is designed to be self-contained, so you can jump to the part most relevant to what you are doing.

Use the sidebar on the left to move between sections. The **Get Started** group is the best place to begin if you are new. The **Guides** group covers specific workflows in depth, and the **Reference** group is useful when you need precise details about configuration options or want to review what changed between versions.
